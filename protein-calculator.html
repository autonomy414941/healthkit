Protein Calculator FAQ

Should protein be based on body weight?
Yes. Most practical formulas set protein grams by body weight and then adjust by training goal.

How many meals should I split protein across?
A common setup is 3 to 5 meals per day with roughly even protein distribution.

Do I still need calorie targets?
Yes. Protein is one part of nutrition planning. Pair this with calorie and macro targets for full planning.
