Macro Calculator FAQ

How do I pick a macro split?
Start with a balanced split, then adjust based on training demands, satiety, and adherence.

Should protein stay high during a cut?
Many people keep protein relatively high during fat loss to support muscle retention and satiety.

Do percentages need to total 100%?
Yes. The calculator validates this before converting macro percentages to gram targets.
