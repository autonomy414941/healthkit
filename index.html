Health Calculator FAQ

Are these calculators free?
Yes, all HealthKit calculators are free and available without signup.

Which calculator should I start with?
Most users start with calories (TDEE), then use macros to split calories, and BMI as a quick screening metric.

Can calculator results replace medical guidance?
No. These tools provide estimates and should be combined with advice from a licensed clinician when needed.
