Water Intake FAQ

How many liters should most adults drink?
Needs vary, but many adults land around 2 to 3.5 liters per day depending on size, activity, and climate.

Should I drink more on workout days?
Yes. Heavy training and sweating usually increase fluid needs beyond your baseline.

What if I have kidney, heart, or endocrine conditions?
Follow your clinician's guidance, because fluid recommendations may differ from general formulas.
