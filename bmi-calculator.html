BMI FAQ

What is BMI?
BMI (Body Mass Index) is weight relative to height. It is useful for population screening but not a diagnosis on its own.

What is the normal BMI range?
For adults, the commonly used healthy range is 18.5 to 24.9.

Why might BMI be misleading?
Muscle mass, body composition, age, and ethnicity can affect how well BMI reflects personal health risk.
